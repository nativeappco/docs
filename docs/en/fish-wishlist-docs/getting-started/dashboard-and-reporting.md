# Dashboard & Reporting

The Fish Wishlist dashboard gives you a real-time overview of wishlist activity across your store — and because all data is stored natively in Shopify metafields, you have more reporting power than you might expect.

* * *

## Dashboard overview

The dashboard shows:

*   **Basic analytics** — wishlist activity over time, filterable by daily periods
*   **Most Wanted products** — your most wishlisted products across the store — useful for buying decisions, restock priorities, and promotional planning
*   **App block status** — the live status of every Fish Wishlist block installed on your store, with a theme selector to check any theme in your account

* * *

## Native customer segmentation

Every customer who uses Fish Wishlist gets a Shopify metafield automatically applied to their profile:

    fish_wishlist_user = true
    

This means you can create Shopify customer segments right now — no third-party tools or custom development required.

**To create a segment:**

1.  Go to **Shopify Admin > Customers > Segments**
2.  Create a new segment with the filter: `Customer metafield: fish_wishlist_user is true`
3.  Use this segment for email campaigns, discounts, loyalty programs, or any other targeting

This works out of the box with Shopify Email, Klaviyo, and any other platform connected to your Shopify customer data.
