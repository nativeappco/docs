# Abandoned Cart Wishlist

Automatically save a customer's abandoned cart products into a dedicated wishlist slot — so you can surface them again when the customer returns.

* * *

## How to enable

1.  Go to **Fish Wishlist > Settings**
2.  Toggle on **Abandoned Cart**

Fish Wishlist uses a Shopify webhook to detect cart abandonment and save those products automatically. No extra configuration required.

* * *

## Displaying it on your store

Use the [Wishlist Page Section](/wishlist-section-on-any-page) to add the Abandoned Cart slot to any page via the theme editor.

**Recommended placements:**

*   **Wishlist drawer** — abandoned cart items appear automatically when the customer opens their wishlist
*   **Dedicated recovery page** — create a standalone page and link to it from abandoned cart emails
*   **Homepage** — surface saved items as a "Pick up where you left off" section for returning customers

* * *

## Combining with email marketing

Pair the Abandoned Cart wishlist slot with your email platform via [Shopify Flow](/shopify-flow-email-marketing) to build a complete recovery sequence:

1.  Customer abandons cart → products saved to wishlist slot automatically
2.  Flow triggers an abandoned cart email with a link to the recovery page
3.  Customer clicks through and sees their saved items ready to add to cart

* * *

## Notes

*   Abandoned cart data is stored at the product variant level
*   The slot updates each time a customer abandons a cart — it reflects their most recent abandonment
*   Customers must have an account for data to be stored persistently
